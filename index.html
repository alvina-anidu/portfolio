Background

Built on Real Results

Starting with building an audience around original music, I learned quickly that good content without a strategy goes nowhere. Through consistent testing and close attention to performance data, I grew a creative catalogue to 2M streams, landed a Reel at 1M views, and drove a single song to 800K Spotify streams with no paid promotion.

That experience led to working with other brands on their content. Since then, my work has expanded into audits, editing projects, and growth strategies across a range of accounts and niches.
